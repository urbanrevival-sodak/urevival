Tylan is a proud member of the Sac and Fox tribe of the Mississippi in Iowa and an affiliated member of the Rosebud Sioux Tribe. Tylan is the treasurer for the South Dakota State University AIAS Chapter. He has a passion for incorporating his indigenous roots into modern, contemporary architecture. He likes to spend time with friends and family, often hunting and fishing in his freetime. He also has a passion for fabrication, making things out of wood in the production lab.

Jack has always loved creating. Not only does it fuel his passion for architecture, but it has motivated him to try different mediums. In his free time, Jack enjoys game development and website design. In fact, he was the one who typed these bios. He is also a partial small business owner of a popular sports team in Green Bay, WI. Jack is the secretary on the South Dakota State University AIAS Board. He is also the co-president of the Department of Architecture’s SAB (Student Advisory Board).

Aliyah has a profound passion for people, history, and culture. She is currently in the final year of her undergraduate studies in Architecture, pursuing a master's degree the following year. At South Dakota State University, Aliyah takes on the role of president for the AIAS Chapter, showcasing her leadership skills and commitment to the field. Her ambition extends beyond the technical aspects of architecture; she aspires to utilize her skills to advocate for individuals through design. Beyond her academic pursuits, Aliyah finds joy in spending time with her family, friends and collecting Legos.

Grady, a vibrant campus figure, holds key positions such as Vice President of AIAS, Student Association Senator at Large, and Vice President of the Men's Soccer Club while also being an active member of SigEp. Outside of leadership roles, Grady cherishes time with friends, indulging in rock climbing, workouts, and outdoor pursuits. His passion extends to software design for building projects, aligning with his attendance at AIA and AIAS events. A dedicated OneWheel enthusiast, Grady finds joy in balancing his active lifestyle. With a love for the "Guardians of the Galaxy'' series, and favorite songs like "Baby Come Back" by Player and "Covet" by Basement, Grady seamlessly blends his diverse interests into a dynamic campus presence.
